Hardware Overview

Our cheese grater is built around a Pololu gearmotor, a custom lead screw mechanism, and a rotary grater blade. The main structure is 3D printed for rapid prototyping and easy customization. Safety was a key concern, so we incorporated a robust enclosure and a spring-loaded cheese pusher to keep hands away from moving parts.
